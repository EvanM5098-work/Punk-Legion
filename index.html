About us...

We are a group of friends, gaming "buddies" if you will. The majority of us started on Playstation, moved around a bit but ended up back on Playstation 4. Unlike most gaming groups, we do not aim to go "Pro". We are simply here to enjoy gaming as it was meant to be enjoyed, with friends! Our youngest member and also the Co-Founder of the group Luhann Marais is the brother of the Leader/Founder, Evan Marais. both of them are driven by gaming and the leader, even created this webpage dedicated solely to the group and his love for gaming. With the new Playstation 5 quickly approaching, we as a group, are extremely excited to see where this new Generation takes us!
